Coronavirus testing at community college canceled Friday due to weather

www.capecodtimes.com

2020-04-03 15:04:27

WEST BARNSTABLE — Coronavirus testing at Cape Cod Healthcare's drive-thru testing site at Cape Cod Community College has been canceled Friday because of weather conditions, according to Barnstable County officials. Testing will resume Saturday morning, and more hours will be added as needed. County officials said a high wind warning and coastal flood warning are in effect for Cape Cod through Friday evening. Northeast wind gusts up to 60 mph are expected for Cape Cod and the Islands
