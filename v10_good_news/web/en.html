Bear Cub Explores Pennsylvania Resident’s Bird Feeders

www.yahoo.com

2020-07-26 15:39:51

On a warm day in the Pennsylvanian village of Tannersville, a bear cub couldn’t decide which bird feeder was “just right”. In this video uploaded to Twitter on July 3, the young animal is seen eating from one of Helen Yanulus’s bird feeders before moving onto the next. Seeing some movement indoors, the bear is seen making a run for it, and climbing down the deck. With a largely undeveloped area near her property, Yanulus is accustomed to seeing a variety of wildlife, but she told Storyful that seeing a young bear climbing “up about a story to the top of the deck to investigate our seeds” is a first.
